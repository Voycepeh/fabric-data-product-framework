# AI-generated lineage and handover (MVP steps 12 and 13)

This workflow maps directly to:
- **Step 12:** AI generated lineage and transformation summary.
- **Step 13:** Handover framework pack.

Users should not manually write lineage step by step. The framework captures transformation summaries where possible, and AI/Copilot drafts readable artifacts.

## Draft artifacts AI/Copilot can produce
- Transformation summary
- Lineage record
- Business explanation
- Handover notes
- Known caveats

## Human responsibility
Humans review and approve the final lineage and handover pack before production release.
