# AI-generated lineage and handover (MVP steps 12 and 13)

## Scope
- **Step 12:** AI generated lineage and transformation summary.
- **Step 13:** Handover framework pack.

Users should not manually write lineage step-by-step where avoidable.

The framework should collect transformation summaries where possible, then AI/Copilot can scan notebook logic and draft:
- Transformation summary
- Lineage record
- Business explanation
- Handover notes
- Known caveats

The human reviews and approves the final handover pack before production handoff.
